## Quantum computing

Quantum computing is an interesting computational paradigm that uses principles from quantum mechanics such as superposition and entanglement. Unlike classical bits, qubits or quantum bits can exist in multiple states at once, which allows quantum computers to solve
complex problems using parallelism much more efficiently.

## Algorithm

An algorithm I find interesting is the Quantum Fourier Transform (QFT), which is a key component in several quantum algorithms such as Shor’s algorithm for integer factorization. It demonstrates how quantum interference can be used to extract useful information from quantum states.


## Quantum SineKAN

SineKAN uses Fourier basis functions, and quantum circuits naturally compute sinusoidal functions through Pauli rotations. Data Encoding in quantum ML is also rotation based. I'd love to make that connection rigorous and efficient.
